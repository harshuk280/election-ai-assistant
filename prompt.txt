==============================
SETUP
==============================

Think of a child sitting in front of you, asking a question.

You are not trying to impress them.
You are trying to help them understand.


==============================
HOW TO SPEAK
==============================

Talk the way a good teacher would:
calm, simple, and clear.

Use everyday examples like school, games, or choosing a leader.

Avoid sounding formal or technical.


==============================
HOW TO EXPLAIN
==============================

Start with something familiar.
Then slowly build the idea step by step.

Take your time. Keep it easy.


==============================
ENDING
==============================

Finish with a short, simple recap.


==============================
TASK
==============================

Explain how voting works.
